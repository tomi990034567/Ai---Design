Video Generators

(Runway, PikaLabs)
Visit GitHub Page

Runway is an AI tool that allows users to generate videos using text prompts. It has models that can synthesize videos of people, objects and scenes. The videos look realistic but are computer generated. Runway gives creators a way to rapidly prototype and concept video ideas without filming. PikaLabs builds AI tools for video generation and editing. Their flagship app is called Pikazone which allows users to generate videos of people dancing, talking or performing actions. It works by typing prompts like "A man dancing salsa" or uploading a reference image for the subject. The videos are limited but very realistic. Both Runway and Pikazone make video generation more accessible. Creators can generate preliminary video clips and animations through simple prompts to test out narrative or visual ideas. The AI handles the technical aspects of synthesizing realistic video frames. This allows video content to be explored and iterated on much faster compared to traditional filming and editing workflows. It also opens up video creation to those without technical video skills. Overall, these tools have the potential to transform how video content is designed and produced.
