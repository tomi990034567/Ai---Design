Going forward, I am interested in further developing my skills with 3D modeling and animation using Blender. Achieving more complex, photorealistic scenes remains a goal. I also aim to integrate AI generation more seamlessly into 3D environments. Continuing to experiment with narrative video generation shows promise. I'd like to explore telling longer, more intricately plotted stories through AI tools. Advancing my video editing abilities will be important for that. Generating original musical compositions and sound design remains an intriguing avenue. As audio models progress, I hope to craft fully immersive virtual worlds and experiences through sound. On the design side, developing comprehensive creative frameworks, toolkits, and best practices for ethical AI integration could help push the field forward. Continuing thoughtful discussions around related issues is worthwhile. I plan to apply the skills and techniques developed through these explorations to both my future career and personal creative pursuits. The ability to thoughtfully direct AI using these tools will serve me well in both professional and artistic contexts going forward.
